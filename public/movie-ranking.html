The Best Movies According to Mega

My top 3 movies of all-time!

Prisoners

This is a thriller movie about a father who is trying to solve a mystery of his missing daugther. My favorite thriller movie.

Pee Mak

This is my favorite comedy horror movie. It is a Thai movie and is one of the best comedy movie out there. If you want a good time and a good laugh, watch this movie. And expect some emotional tears towards the end of the movie.

Interstellar

This is an epic sci-fi movie. This movie is about time and space and it definetly the kind of movie that makes you use your brain (hint: physics lover).
